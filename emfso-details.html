EMFSO-Attendance Android App

As the Vice President & Director of Indoor Program for Electric Model Flyers of Southern Ontario (EMFSO), I was responsible for managing the organization's interactions with the York Region District School Board for the use of their indoor gyms. Part of those responsibilities were taking attendance and keeping metrics for the indoor flight. While it was done for many years using pen and paper I created this app at the time for taking attendance for EMFSO events in indoor and outdoor settings.
The app provides several advantages over pen and paper; you wouldn't have to worry about losing that day's attendance sheet as it was stored on your phone, and it was much more convenient when carrying planes to/from the venue and your car.
